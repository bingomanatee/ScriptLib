Choose your personality

There are no "good" or "bad" personalities; personality determines how good a match you are for a given role.

You don't have to have a "Balanced" personality.

  • If your personality is too mild, you aren't going to be a great fit for any leading roles.
  • If your personality is too strong, you'll be perfect for a very few roles, but have a narrower range.
